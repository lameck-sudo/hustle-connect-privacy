Welcome to Hustle Connect Kenya. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information while using our platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Picture (Optional)
  • County and Location Information
  • Skills and Professional Details
  • Gig Listings and Applications
  • Messages Sent Through The App
  • Device Information and Usage Statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Create and manage accounts
  • Connect workers with clients
  • Enable secure communication
  • Display relevant gigs and opportunities
  • Improve app functionality
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Provide customer support

3. Location Services

If enabled, location information may be used to display nearby gigs, improve search results, and enhance the user experience. Location access is optional.

4. Data Storage & Security

Data is securely stored using Google Firebase services. While we take reasonable security measures, no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

5. User Content

Information you submit, including gig posts, applications, ratings, reviews, and profile information, may be visible to other users as required by app functionality.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Realtime Database
  • Firebase Storage
  • Google Play Services

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared when:

  • Required by law
  • Investigating fraud or abuse
  • Protecting users and the platform

8. Account Deletion

Users may request account deletion. Upon deletion, personal information will be removed where technically possible and legally permitted.

9. Children's Privacy

Hustle Connect Kenya is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
